Our Rehearsal Space

Our rehearsal space in Banjara Hills serves as the creative hub for SHOi. This is where we bring scripts to life, develop characters, and perfect our performances.

We welcome visitors during our rehearsal hours to observe our process, meet our team, and learn more about our community. If you're interested in joining us or attending a rehearsal, please contact us in advance.

Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions about SHOi

How can I attend a SHOi performance?

You can book tickets for our shows through our website, by calling us, or by visiting our rehearsal space. Members get priority booking and discounts.

Can I participate in SHOi productions as an actor?

Yes! We welcome new talent. Please contact us to schedule an audition or attend one of our open casting calls announced on our social media.

Do you offer theatre workshops?

Yes, we regularly conduct workshops on acting, script writing, and stagecraft. Check our website or social media for upcoming workshop announcements.

How can I support SHOi as a non-performer?

There are many ways to support us! You can become a member, volunteer for backstage work, help with promotions, or sponsor our productions.
