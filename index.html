Octopus Limited ESTD 2024

15 Oct 2024 Hyderabad

"Octopus Limited Estd. 2024" is a 2024 adaptation of Mohit Chattopadhyay's classic Bengali play, "Octopus Limited," which was originally written in 1998. The play is a satirical look at corporate organizations and societal changes, specifically an unmarried couple who are offered a deal by an agent from a company.

Hamlet Ya Nahi?

5 Apr 2025 Bangalore

"Hamlet Ya Nahi" is a modern Hindi drama play, not the original Shakespearean tragedy, but an adaptation that centers on an actor auditioning for the role of Hamlet. It explores the blurred lines between performance and reality as the actor delves deeper into the character, leading to a darker, suspenseful story of obsession and betrayal
