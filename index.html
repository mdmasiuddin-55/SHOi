About SHOi

Discover our journey, mission, and the passionate team behind Hyderabad's premier Bengali theatre community

Our Journey

Initiated in April 2024, Shoi is a group of Bengali artists who, having had to move away from home to earn bread (and for other reasons), collaborate and practice theatre.

Shoi, in Bangla means. signiture. In the OTP and Facebook era, the signature still prevails and holds it's value. It still remains an identity of oneself. We beleive, our work is our identity. It is unique, just like a signiture Interestingly. Shoi also means friends.

Our wider aim is to promote the culture of 'Bangla Group Theatre' in Hyderabad. Anyone with the intention to learn theatre, and work on the craft can join us. That's all you need, intrest and intention

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for Bengali cultural expression in Hyderabad and beyond, fostering artistic talent and creating memorable theatrical experiences that resonate with diverse audiences.

Our Values

  • Cultural Preservation: Honoring and promoting Bengali traditions
  • Artistic Excellence: Striving for quality in every performance
  • Community Building: Creating a supportive network of artists and enthusiasts
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming people from all backgrounds to experience Bengali culture
