Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership program

How do I become a member?

Simply click on the "Join Now" button for your preferred membership plan and complete the registration process. You can also visit us during our rehearsal hours to sign up in person.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. The remaining value of your current membership will be applied toward the upgraded plan.

Do you offer family or group memberships?

Currently, we offer individual memberships only. However, we do provide special discounts for group bookings of 5 or more people for our shows.

How do I renew my membership?

We'll send you a renewal reminder one month before your membership expires. You can renew online or in person at our rehearsal space.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept online payments through UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking. For in-person registration, we accept cash and digital payments.
